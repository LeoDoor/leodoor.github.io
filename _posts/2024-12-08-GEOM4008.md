# Error Analysis
- Investigated sources of error in Shuttle Radar Topography Mission (SRTM) Digital Elevation Models (DEMs).
- Compared the strengths and weaknesses of different interpolation methods to assess their impact on data accuracy.

# Kriging Interpolation Tutorial
- Developed a tutorial on **Kriging Interpolation Comparison** using Alberta weather station elevation data in **System for Automated Geoscientific Analysis (SAGA GIS)**.
- Analyzed how data density impacts interpolation accuracy and presented findings visually.
- Focused on the benefits and limitations of the kriging technique for elevation modeling.
